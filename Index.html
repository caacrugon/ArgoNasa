NASA Space Apps Challenge - 2025 Edition

A World Away: Hunting for Exoplanets with AI

ArgoNasa fuses citizen science with explainable AI to uncover new worlds hidden in NASA's exoplanet archives. Explore how our lightweight toolkit empowers communities to detect, validate, and share planetary discoveries in near real time.

Mission Focus

We tackle the "A World Away" challenge by translating complex light-curve data into intuitive stories. Our platform guides volunteers through the hunt: from signal cleaning and transit identification to AI-assisted candidate grading and science-ready reports.

Solution Snapshot

  • AI Co-Pilot Hybrid neural filters spotlight likely exoplanet transits while surfacing confidence metrics anyone can interpret.
  • Citizen Science Workflow Guided missions turn raw TESS and Kepler data into community-driven discoveries, complete with learning badges and feedback loops.
  • Insight Hub A storytelling dashboard combines visual analytics, mission milestones, and exportable summaries for judges and partner scientists.

On the Horizon

Next iterations expand multilingual onboarding, integrate open catalogs for cross-verification, and publish an open API so classrooms and labs can plug into the discovery stream without friction.
